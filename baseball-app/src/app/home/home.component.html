Currently this component doesn't have any data to display. Ideally it would display info about upcoming matches/games as well as scores of previous matches/games.
As per the requirements, the workflow of this app is as follows:

(1) "Teams" menu item will take you the teams view, where you'll be able to see all the current MLB teams.
(2) In the teams view, selecting or clicking on a team will take you to the team details view.
(3) From the team details view, you'll be able to see the team's current/active roster.
(4) Once a player has been selected from the roster, you'll be taken to the player details view.
(5) In the player details view you'll be able to see the player's Fielding, Hitting and Pitching stats. I decided to only retrieve the stats for last the 3 years (i.e 2019, 2018, 2017).


The "Players" menu item will take you to the players view where you can see all the players that are currently active. Clicking on a player will take you to the player details view.

The "Venues" menu item will take you to the venues view where it displays the ballpark from each MLB venue.
